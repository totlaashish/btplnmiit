UG Admission 2018

Admission Policy 2018

Admissions to all the Undergraduate Programmes will be through the following modes:


a) JEE (Main) Mode

Eligibility Criteria
  • The applicant must be appearing in JEE (Main) 2018 Examination conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).
  • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 60% Marks or equivalent Grade in Class 10th Examination.
  • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 60% Marks or equivalent Grade in Class 12th Examination.
  • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 60% Marks or Equivalent Grade in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in Class 12th Examination.

Selection Procedure
  • The LNMIIT will draw a Merit List, solely on the basis of total marks obtained in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in JEE (Main) 2018 Examination, of only those applicants who have applied to the LNMIIT for admission and fulfill the Eligibility Criteria mentioned above.
  • A minimum Cut-off individually for Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as obtained in the JEE (Main) Examination, as well as the total JEE (Main) Score shall be considered while preparing the Merit List.
  • The admission, in this category, will be based on the Merit List drawn by the LNMIIT considering the above mentioned criteria.

Application Fee

 Rs. 1600/- (Rupees Sixteen Hundred Only) Non-refundable

b) Direct Mode

Only for Central and State Board Toppers

Eligibility Criteria
  • Top 2 students in Class 12th Examination of all those Central and State Boards in India who publish authorized Merit List indicating ranks.
  • Top 10 students in Class 12th Examination from the Merit List of the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE), provided that official Merit List mentioning such ranks is published.
  • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 60% Marks or equivalent Grade in Class 10th Examination.

Selection Procedure
  • The applicant needs to submit the Application Form as per the specified format.
  • The applicant needs to submit the proof of the Rank during Counselling and Registration process.

Application Fee

 Rs. 1600/- (Rupees Sixteen Hundred Only) Non-refundable

c) DASA (Direct Admission to  Students from Abroad) Mode

Only for Foreign Nationals/ PIOs/ NRIs

Residential Requirements
  • The applicant must be a Foreign National/ a Person of Indian Origin residing outside India.
  • A Foreign National may have completed the qualifying examination in any country (including India).
  • However, for a person of Indian origin, s/he must have completed her/his Class 11th and 12th or equivalent from outside India.

Academic Eligibility
  • The applicant must have a minimum valid score of 1440 in SAT Subject Tests (Subjects: Mathematics Level 2, Physics, and Chemistry).
  • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 60% Marks or equivalent Grade in Class 10th Examination.
  • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 60% Marks or equivalent Grade in Class 12th Examination.
  • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 60% Marks or Equivalent Grade in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class 12th Examination or equivalent.

Selection Procedure
  • The LNMIIT will draw a Merit List, on the basis of SAT Subject Score, of only those applicants who have applied to LNMIIT for admission and fulfill the Eligibility Criteria mentioned above.

Application Fee
  • $ 100 (US Dollar One Hundred Only) Non-refundable

d) Lateral Entry Mode

For students of other Institutes after 1st & 2nd Semester

Eligibility Criteria
  • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 60% Marks or equivalent Grade in Class 10th Examination.
  • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 60% Marks or equivalent Grade in Class 12th Examination.
  • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 60% Marks or Equivalent Grade in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class 12th Examination.
  • If the applicant is applying after 1st Semester:
    • The applicant must have appeared in JEE (Main) Examination in the current academic year (2018-19) conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).
    • The applicant must have a JEE (Main) Score equal or above the Branch-wise Cut-off Marks of the current academic year (LNMIIT Merit List 2018).

  • If the applicant is applying after 2nd Semester:

    • The applicant must have appeared in JEE (Main) Examination in the previous academic year (2017-18) conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).
    • The applicant must have a JEE (Main) Score equal or above the Branch-wise Cut-off Marks of the previous academic year (LNMIIT Merit List 2017).
  • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 75% Marks or equivalent Grade in Semester I or II, whichever is applicable.

Selection Procedure
  • The shortlisted applicants, based on the above mentioned Eligibility Criteria, need to appear for a personal interview conducted by the Lateral Admission Committee.

Application Fee

 Rs. 1600/- (Rupees Sixteen Hundred Only) Non-refundable

Note :

  • Admission Policy and Eligibility Criteria for Admissions to B. Tech.-M. Tech. (5-Year Integrated Dual Degree) Programmes is exactly same as that of the B. Tech. Programme.
  • The Institute has no provision for either Capitation Fee or Management Quota.

  • Medical-cum-Accidental Insurance Charges shall be levied yearly on actual basis from 2nd Semester Onwards.
Scholarship Policy 2018

Merit based Scholarship

  • Scholarships are awarded to the meritorious students based on the following criteria:
    1. 1st semester: The following students are eligible for scholarships in the first semester.
      a) Top rankers of JEE Advanced (2018) exam
      b) Students admitted via the “Meritorious Students of Rajasthan State Board” mode.
    2. 2nd semester onwards: Academic performance at LNMIIT (CGPA >= 9.0).

Merit-Cum- Means Scholarship and BPL Scholarship

Objective: To foster an environment of competitive excellence and support the education of economically marginal sections of the society.

Merit-cum- Means Scholarship:

Quantum of scholarship: 50% of the tuition fee to top 10 eligible students
Merit Criterion (First Semester): Minimum JEE (Advanced) Rank: 15000

OR

Minimum JEE (Main) PCM total Marks: 175

Second Semester onwards: CGPA ≥8.5 and no backlogs

Means Criterion: Gross annual parental income (both parents) from all the sources Rs. ≤5,00,000.00

Documents:

  1. Income proof (Income certificate issued by competent authority)*
  2. Copy of ITR of previous financial year (applicable for tax payers only)
  3. Affidavit signed by both parents and attested by first class magistrate
  4. One year savings account statement
  5. Details of the property owned (personal/ancestral)

* For salaried parents proof of income will be provided by the head of the department/organization. In computing their gross annual income all components of their salary excluding the employers' share of PF/EPF/NPS contribution will be included.

For non-salaried parents Attested copies of an Annual Income Certificate for 2015-16 from District Authority like S.D.O./B.D.O./Tahalsidar, Anchal Officer, Executive Officer of Municipal Corporation/ Gram Panchayet etc. will be applicable.

Process of Award : A screening committee will look into the merit of all the cases and may ask the applicants to appear for an interview, if so required. For the renewal of scholarship applicants will be required to submit the income certificate/copy of ITR showing the gross annual income of the previous year. They need not submit the other documents. In the cases of renewal, 10% increase in income will be acceptable for the award of scholarship.

Note : In case of academic under-performance or securing a backlog the scholarship will be withdrawn and will be reinstated only after the applicant fulfills minimum merit criterion.

BPL Scholarship:

Quantum of Scholarship: 100% of the tuition fee
First Semester : All the BPL students admitted are eligible.
Second Semester onwards: BPL students with CGPA of ≥6.0 and no backlogs

Note: In case of academic under performance of the student the following will apply:

  1. If the CGPA fall below 6.0 the student will be issued a warning and will be expected to work harder to bring his/her CGPA to 6.0 within one semesters time. For that one semester s/he will be given scholarship. However, failing this, the scholarship will be withdrawn and reinstated only after, and with retrospective effect, the student achieves the CGPA of ≥6.0.
  2. In case of a backlog, the scholarship will be withdrawn and reinstated only after, and with retrospective effect, the backlogs are cleared.

Documents:

  1. A copy of BPL Card
  2. Affidavit signed by both parents and attested by first class magistrate

Process of Award: A screening committee will look into the merit of all the cases and may ask the applicants to appear for an interview, if so required. BPL scholarship will automatically get renewed provided the beneficiary fulfills the minimum CGPA and no backlog requirement.

Note:

  1. No student will be eligible for more than one scholarship.
  2. All the scholarships are designed to compensate for the tuition fee.
  3. In the case of Merit-cum- means and BPL scholarships, if a student is getting scholarship from some other source, and if it is equal to his/her tuition fee, the student will not be given any LNMIIT scholarship. However, if the scholarship from the other source falls short of the tuition fee, LNMIIT will compensate for the remaining tuition fee, subject to the upper limit of the scholarship applicable in an individual's case.
  4. Scholarship is not a matter of right. It is a privilege offered by the institute to enhance internal competition and thereby academic excellence, and support the needy. The institute reserves the right to eliminate or increase/decrease the quantum of scholarship and change the policy conditions.

Scholarship policies are reviewed by the Academic Council and Governing Council from time to time.

Refund Policy 2018

First Semester Fee of B. Tech./ B. Tech.-M. Tech. (Integrated Dual-Degree) Programme

  1. The applicant need to fill the Refund Form by login to the Admission Portal using his/ her login credentials. Without the formal refund application, the refund cases will not be processed.
  2. If the Institute fails to offer a confirmed seat to an applicant and if s/he remains in the Waiting List throughout the admission process, the full Provisional Fee of Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Thousand Only) will be refunded.
  3. Applicants getting a confirmed seat and failing to deposit the remaining Balance Fee of Rs. 1,53,000/- (Rupees One Lakh Fifty Three Thousand Only) by the specified deadline as mentioned in the Admission Offer Letter by any reason, his/ her Admission Offer will be cancelled. In such case, Rs. 24,000/- (Rupees Twenty Four Thousand Only) will be refunded after deduction of Rs. 1,000/- (Rupees One Thousand Only).
  4. Once the applicant deposits the Balance Fee within the stipulated deadline, s/he will be considered as an enrolled student in the offered Programme of study.
  5. Last Date of Admission for all the UG Programmes shall be 30th July 2018, unless otherwise specified by a statutory regulatory body or the Institute through its notification on Admission Portal.
  6. All the refund cases of enrolled students will be processed in accordance with formally-notified Last Date of Admission.
  7. If the enrolled student selects to withdraw from the Programme of study, the four-tier system for the refund of fees as per the UGC Notification D.O. No. 1-3/2007 (CPP-II) dated 6th December 2016 shall be followed.

  10. In case of refund, the no. of days will be counted from the date of refund application received through online portal or the date of confirmation of payment of fees received from the Bank, whichever is later.
  11. The refund requests made through email, phone or any other mode, other than the specified in (1), will not be entertained.
  12. Enrolled student must provide following information along with the refund request: (i) Name of Account Holder, (ii) Relation with the Student, (iii) Account No., (iv) Bank Name, (v) Bank Branch and Address, (vi) IFSC Code of the Bank.

  14. The University will not be held responsible for any disputes or other matters arising due to the completeness or correctness of the particulars provided in the refund request. The student unconditionally agrees that the particulars provided are correct in all respects and will not make the University party to any disputes which may arise due to furnishing of such particulars.
  15. The date of receipt of complete application for admission cancellation will be treated as date of request. Delay or non-receipt of request shall be responsibility of the enrolled student.
  16. The University has right to cancel the admission of any student, if any student fails to pay the fees by due date or fails to submit mandatory documents as mentioned in respective Provisional Admission Letter. In such cases, refund, if any, shall be governed as per the above mentioned policy.
  17. Once the refund application is received, the admission will be withdrawn. For the admitted students after academic registration, the refund application will be processed only after receiving all the applicable ‘No Dues’ certificate from all the relevant Units/ Departments/ Centres/ Hostel/ Library/ Mess of the University and after receiving the ID card and original fee receipt.
  18. Application fee of Rs. 1,600/- (Rupees Sixteen Hundred Only), once remitted, shall not be refunded under any circumstances.
  19. The courts situated at Jaipur, Rajasthan shall have exclusive Jurisdiction for all disputes.
  20. The refund information given above is indicative only and The LNMIIT, Jaipur reserves the right to make changes as per the notification received from the concerned statutory/ regulatory authorities from time to time. Any changes in the above will be updated on the Admission Portal.

Refund Policy (in case of online payments – double/ failed transactions, credited to the Institute):

  • A transaction will be deemed successful only after the funds are received by the Institute. The online payments are not collected by the Institute directly but by the Service Provider (Payment Gateway). Once payment is received by the Service Provider in cleared funds, the Service Provider will contact the Institute to confirm the details of the successful payment made by the applicant, or third party making payment. On receipt of this confirmation, the Institute will confirm to the applicant that the payment has been received and accepted by the Institute. In the event of the applicant does not receive confirmation within 2 working days of making the payment, it is the responsibility of the applicant to check with the Institute that the payment has been accepted or not.
  • The applicant shall remain responsible for the fees until such time as the confirmation as referred to above is received from the Institute in relation to each of these and any outstanding amounts owed to the Institute.
  • The refund of a particular transaction will be made only in case the applicant has paid online more than once for the same admission. The processing of refunds in these cases requires the verification, and at times remittance as well, by the Payment Gateway. Hence, time delays in some cases may not be under the control of the Institution.
  • The refund applications will be processed and responded to only after proper verification.

Security (Online Transactions):

All payment details which are entered through the payment gateway are encrypted when the applicant, or third party making payment, enters them. Communications to and from the Service Provider’s site are encrypted. The Institute shall not be liable for any failure by the applicant or third party making payment of application processing fees to properly protect data from being seen on their screen by other persons or otherwise obtained by such persons, during the online payment process or in respect of any omission to provide accurate information in the course of the online payment process.
